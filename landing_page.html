Overview

ECIS is a simple and affordable way for startups and emerging companies to find and apply for government grants and incentives to support cash flow and grow your business. We’re a secure, cloud based platform, built and backed by PwC and we’ve collectively helped hundreds of Australian early stage companies access more than $30mil in grants and incentives to fuel their growth.
